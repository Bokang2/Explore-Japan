Experience the Beauty of Japan

Japan the country

Founded on February 11, 660 BC

Japan is an island country in East Asia. It is situated in the northwest Pacific Ocean and is bordered on the west by the Sea of Japan, extending from the Sea of Okhotsk in the north toward the East China Sea, Philippine Sea, and Taiwan in the south.

Four Fun Facts About Japan

1. Shibuya Crossing

Japan is home to the world's busiest pedestrian crossing, located in the Shibuya district of Tokyo. It's called the Shibuya Crossing, and it's an incredible sight to see hundreds of people crossing the street at once from all directions.

2. Sake Vending Machine

The Japanese love vending machines, and you can find them on almost every street corner selling everything from hot drinks to fresh eggs. In fact, there are even vending machines that sell beer and sake.

3. Cherry Blossoms

Japan is known for its beautiful cherry blossoms, which bloom for just a few weeks every spring. During this time, people gather in parks for flower viewing parties, where they eat, drink, and enjoy the scenery.

4. The Tokyo Skytree

The Tokyo Skytree, the tallest tower in the world, stands at 634 meters tall, making it twice as tall as the Eiffel Tower in Paris
